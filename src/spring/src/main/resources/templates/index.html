The genesis

Our team believes in a world without fake identities on the Internet. A place where 
bureaucracy is no longer on paper; where cyberbullying with non genuine accounts is eliminated to build a better community. And that is the origin of the 'AuthMe' project.
  

The AuthMe project features

Secure

All security precautions are taken to ensure that the properties of a digital identity remain intact and are only accessible to the rightful owner and authorized applications.

Authentic

The software uses cutting-edge technologies such as AI to digitalize existing government infrastructures and eliminate fake identities on the Internet.

Fast

Our scalable solution utilizes high-performant principles to guarantee a convenient and flawless experience.
